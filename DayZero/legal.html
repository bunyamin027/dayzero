Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

DayZero ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled within the DayZero iOS application.

1. Information Collection and Use

DayZero is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All information you enter into the app, including event titles, dates, and notes, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData technology.

2. Calendar and Photo Access

Calendar: DayZero requests access to your iOS Calendar to allow you to import events. This data is read locally and never leaves your device.

Photos & Camera: Access to your photo library and camera is used only to allow you to set custom backgrounds for your countdowns. These images stay on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

DayZero does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs. Your usage of the app is completely private.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security features (Face ID, Touch ID, Passcode, etc.). We recommend keeping your device updated.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
